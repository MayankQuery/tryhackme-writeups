# Additional Notes on Cyber Kill Chain

The Cyber Kill Chain is widely used in SOC operations and incident response.
Below is a quick summary of attacker and defender views for each phase:

Reconnaissance:
- Attacker: gather intel
- Defender: limit exposure

Weaponisation:
- Attacker: prepare malware
- Defender: detect payloads

Delivery:
- Attacker: send payload
- Defender: secure communications

Exploitation:
- Attacker: exploit weaknesses
- Defender: patch and harden

Installation:
- Attacker: persistence
- Defender: EDR, FIM

C2:
- Attacker: remote control
- Defender: detect abnormal traffic

Actions on Objectives:
- Attacker: final goals
- Defender: SIEM + IR
