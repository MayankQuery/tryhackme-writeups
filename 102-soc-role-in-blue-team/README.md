## Overview
This repository contains notes, a quick cheatsheet, and task writeups from the TryHackMe room **"SOC Role in Blue Team"**. The room explains how Security Operations Centers (SOCs) fit into an organization, the responsibilities across different SOC tiers (L1, L2, L3), and career growth paths for security analysts.

---

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the Blue Team concept and its purpose.  
- Learn where a SOC sits within a company's security and organizational structure.  
- Identify roles & responsibilities across SOC tiers and related teams (IR, Threat Intelligence, DFIR, Red Team).  
- Discover skills and certifications useful for career progression from SOC L1 to senior roles.
