# Phishing Basics – Cheatsheet

---

## 🎯 Types of Phishing

- Email phishing
- Spear phishing
- Whaling
- Clone phishing

---

## 🧠 Psychological Triggers

- Urgency
- Authority
- Fear
- Curiosity
- Trust

---

## 🧪 Phishing Workflow

1. Gather target information
2. Craft convincing message
3. Deliver via email or link
4. Capture credentials or deploy payload

---

## 🛠️ Social Engineering Toolkit (SET)

Start SET:
```bash
sudo setoolkit
```
Common options:

Credential Harvester

Website Cloner

Email attack vectors

🔎 Detection Tips

Check sender email

Look for spelling errors

Verify links before clicking

Be cautious of urgency

🛡️ Prevention

Security awareness training

Email filtering

Multi-factor authentication

Zero trust approach
