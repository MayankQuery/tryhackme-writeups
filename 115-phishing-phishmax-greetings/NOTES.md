## 🔹 Phishing vs Spam

Phishing:
- Targeted
- Malicious intent
- Credential theft or malware

Spam:
- Bulk messages
- Mostly marketing
- Low risk

---

## 🔹 Common Phishing Goals

- Credential theft
- Malware delivery
- Data exfiltration
- Financial fraud

---

## 🔹 Key Phishing Techniques

### Impersonation
- Pretending to be a trusted person or service

---

### Social Engineering
- Urgency
- Fear
- Authority
- Curiosity

---

### Typosquatting
- Fake domains (e.g., goggle.com)

---

### Punycode
- Unicode characters replacing real letters

---

### Spoofing
- Fake sender identity
- Check:
  - SPF
  - DKIM
  - DMARC

---

### Malicious Attachments
- HTML / HTA files
- Can execute scripts

---

## 🔹 Modern Phishing Trends

- Use of legitimate platforms (Google Drive, OneDrive)
- Fake login pages
- Credential harvesting instead of malware

---

## 🔹 Indicators of Phishing

- Suspicious sender domain
- Urgent language
- Requests for sensitive data
- Mismatched links
- Failed authentication checks

---

## 🧠 Key Takeaway

Phishing detection requires careful analysis of intent, sender authenticity, and subtle technical indicators.
