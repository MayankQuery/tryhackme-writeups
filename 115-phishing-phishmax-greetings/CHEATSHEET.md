## 🔍 What to Check in Emails

- Sender email address
- Domain spelling
- Email headers
- Links and attachments

---

## 📧 Email Header Analysis

Key fields:
- SPF
- DKIM
- DMARC
- Return-Path

---

## ⚠️ Red Flags

- Urgent requests
- Unusual sender domain
- Requests for credentials
- Suspicious attachments
- Grammar mistakes

---

## 🧠 Social Engineering Signals

- Urgency ("Act now")
- Authority ("Manager request")
- Fear ("Account suspended")
- Curiosity

---

## 🌐 Domain Tricks

### Typosquatting
- github.com → githib.com

### Punycode
- Fake Unicode characters

---

## 📎 Malicious Attachments

- .html
- .hta
- .exe
- .zip

---

## 🛡️ Prevention

- Verify sender identity
- Enable MFA
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Educate users

---

## 🎯 SOC Tip

Always analyze:
1. Intent
2. Sender authenticity
3. Technical indicators
